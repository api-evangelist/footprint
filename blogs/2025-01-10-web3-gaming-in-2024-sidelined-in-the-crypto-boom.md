The 2024 gaming sector saw daily active users surge over 300% to 6.58 million and market cap rise 60.5% to $31.8 billion, yet it underperformed other crypto sectors, ranking 8th in growth behind memecoins (421.1%) and AI projects (168.0%). Trading volume reached $5.2 billion (up 18.5%) while transactions declined 30.3%; WAX led in transactions (1.8 billion), BNB Chain dominated trading volume (23.1%), and opBNB led daily active users (2.2 million). Traditional gaming companies including Ubisoft, Square Enix, and Sony made strategic blockchain entries.
