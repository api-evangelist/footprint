November 2024 gaming market cap surged 79.1% to $35.1 billion amid Bitcoin's historic rally, with daily active users reaching 5.5 million (up 15.7%) and daily trading volume up 79.4% to $11.9 million. Active games reached 1,696 (up 4.6%), with BNB Chain, Polygon, and Ethereum holding 20.9%, 15.4%, and 13.4% game distribution shares; opBNB led in daily active users. The report highlights tension between crypto-native appeal and mainstream adoption, noting Off The Grid's strategy of positioning blockchain as optional rather than central.
